Research
Since November 2025

Research Fellow in Perspective-Aware Language Modelling

I joined the Department of Computer Science at the University of Turin as a Research Fellow on a perspective-aware language modelling project developed in partnership with Amazon Alexa. My work focuses on multilingual NLP pipelines for pragmatically constrained dialogue, irony, perspective-aware modelling and human-centred evaluation.
