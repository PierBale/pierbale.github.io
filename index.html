About Me

Pier Balestrucci

Google Scholar GitHub LinkedIn

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Bari and a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Turin.

Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Turin, specializing in Computational Linguistics. My research focuses on dialogue systems and natural language generation, with an emphasis on accessibility and human-centered approaches.

I collaborate with the Polin Laboratory at the Department of Mathematics on projects aimed at improving accessibility through innovative AI solutions.

As part of my doctoral journey, I am currently conducting a research internship at UFAL, Charles University in Prague. Here, I am delving deeper into language model generation techniques, advancing the integration of LLMs with human-centered applications.
