NoVAGraphS: Non-Visual Access to Graphical Structures

I am currently involved in the NoVAGraphS project, which focuses on improving accessibility for visually impaired students in STEM education. This research is conducted in collaboration with Laboratorio Polin, aiming to create innovative tools and methodologies for accessing graphical structures non-visually.
